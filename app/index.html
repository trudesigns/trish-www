my process

“There is a method to my madness.”

- Shakespeare

I take whatever concept at hand and I research my audience, I then start sketching wireframes, once the concept is in play I put my design into a mockup and work on the interactions and over all visual design, I then create a live prototype to deliver the project. Once the completed project goes live I gather the data and analytics from the users interaction and I then make any modifications to the design based on facts.
