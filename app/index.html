my process

“There is a method to my madness.”

- Shakespeare

I take whatever concept I have at hand and then I research my audience, I start off with sketching wireframes, once the concept is in play I put my design into a digital mockup and work on the interactions and over all visual design, I then create a live prototype to deliver the project. Once the completed project goes live I gather the data and analytics from the users interaction and I then make any modifications to the design based on facts and I make sure all data and the content is all correlating together for the SEO.

TADA you got yourself a beautiful master piece after a boat load of expresso shots.
