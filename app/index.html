About Me

about me

I consume design theory and art like a kid in a digital candy shop. The sight of a new typeface or charming color palette inspires experimental fervor. I have a voracious appetite for new interactive technologies. I am a designer with many styles, I can be fun and playful with bright colors pallets, funky edges and textures or professional and conservative with a clean and minimalistic approach.

I find creativity in everything I touch, with the desire to impact the crowd with amazing feelings when they look at my work.

I also like going to the beach and watching TED whenever I catch some free time.

my process

I take whatever concept at hand and I research my audience, I start with color pallets, style elements and fonts, then jump into content structure better know as Information architecture, once I structure the hyarchy by card sorting what is most important to deliver the message, I then start sketching wireframes, once the concept is in play I put my design into a mockup and work on the interactions and over all visual design, I then create a live prototype to deliver the project. Once I work with Developers and the completed project goes live I gather the data and analytics from the users interaction and I then make any modifications to the design based on facts.
